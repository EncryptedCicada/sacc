About Us

Sports Mentorship and Adventure Club has been providing students the much required amalgamation of sports and adventure activities along with early guidance. BIT Mesra firmly believes in all-round development. Since 2010, Sports Mentorship and Adventure Club leads the way by assuming responsibility for the physical and mental wellbeing of all our students by organizing sports-related events and encouraging participation.

“Winning isn't everything--but wanting to win is.”

About Our Events

Our events have been the most ideal rendezvous for anyone and everyone who have the tiniest of interest in sports Apart from making students physically active, the activities are sedulously organized to help them become more focused and train their minds to accommodate ideas of team spirit, leadership and accountability.

“The miracle isn't that I finished. The miracle is that I had the courage to start.”

About Mentorship Drive

Mentorship Drives are meticulously designed in the form of seminars, webinars and interactive sessions with our Alumni and other inspirational personalities with a sincere goal of guiding the students to discover their passion, purpose and direction. Also, at a point of time where everyone has been a victim of the pandemic and its crisis, we made sure that there is no hindrance in the club’s activities. From organizing online webinars and e-sports fest, SMAC has proved and will forever continue to prove its mettle

“Persistence can change failure into extraordinary achievement.”
